About Us

Sprout & Grow is a family owned greenhouse business that was established in 1982 in Raleigh, North Carolina. We strive to provide the best plants to anyone looking to decorate their home or start a new hobby, all while keeping good practices. We prioritize in helping people choose the plant that will bring content to their lives. A plant is more than just a plant!
